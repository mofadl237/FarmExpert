Dairy Farming System

We were the first cow monitoring system with an ear sensor for cows. We have a lot of dairy farming experience, did years of research, testing, developing, and we update our system’s hardware and software bi-weekly. Our technology gives you extremely accurate information about your herd’s fertility, health, and nutritional status.

The ear sensor fits around an official animal identification RFID tag and stays put thanks to an effective attachment mechanism. In just a single action, you can place the sensor in a cow’s ear. It’s easy to remove and attach to another cow, too. And there’s no need for recharging batteries and adjusting collars. Easy does it!

Smart Cow Tracking System

Our cow tracking system makes dairy farming easier. You’ll be able to check your herd’s well-being, anytime and anywhere. Our always up-to-date cow monitoring system gives you the opportunity to check how your herd is doing, whether you have 20 or 20,000 cows.

Receive alerts on the fertility, health, and nutrition of your cows. Dive into cow data by just opening the CowManager app on your laptop or your smartphone or tablet (iOS or Android). You’ll have all your real-time cow information at your fingertips, wherever you are. Use the MultiView functionality so your herdsperson, veterinarian, nutritionist, and other associates have remote access to selected data as well. That way, you can stay ahead of the herd and use the system to monitor cattle together.

Deep Dive Into Data

The dashboard is an easy-to-use visual representation of your real-time data to simplify your daily routine. All relevant alerts and insights are displayed in one place through various widgets, including the farmxpert window.

The drag-and-drop dashboard is fully customizable and displays information from all modules conveniently in one place. Make your own overview of information that’s most important to you and deep dive into cow data with just a single click. All alerts that require attention are available at just a glance on your home screen.

Herd Management System Integrations

Together we can do better. That’s why we integrate with herd management systems all over the world. Some systems even have a two-way interface with CowManager. It gives dairy farmers the opportunity to see data simultaneously in both systems.

When calculating alerts, we use data from both systems. More information means more detailed alerts and an overall better-informed decision process. These individual cow alerts can drive drafting/sorting gates, making herd management that much easier.
